Research Interests

I focus on earthquake source physics and fault strength evolution in relation to a range of lithospheric deformation modes, including seismic and aseismic slip events, using numerical models, theoretical analysis and observations from natural earthquakes. My research incorporates the advanced open-access software (e.g. SeisSol, Specfem) for simulating dynamic earthquake ruptures for both crustal and subduction earthquakes. I am also interested in developing high-performance computing toolkits for Sequences of Earthquakes and Aseismic Slip (SEAS).
